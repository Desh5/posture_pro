About

The Smart Posture Monitoring System helps users maintain a healthy sitting posture during long working hours. The system monitors posture angle, sitting direction, and sitting duration in real time and alerts the user when posture becomes unhealthy.

This system is also useful for patients undergoing physiotherapy or recovering from spinal problems. By providing posture feedback and alerts, it helps maintain proper spinal alignment and promotes better sitting habits.
